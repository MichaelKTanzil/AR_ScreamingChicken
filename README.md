# Screaming Chicken

> An AR-based mobile game where your voice is the controller — scream your way to the top!

---

## Description

**Screaming Chicken** is an augmented reality (AR) mobile game that turns your real-world environment into a playground. Using your device's camera, the game places interactive blocks on flat surfaces around you. The catch? The only way to control your chicken and reach higher blocks is by **screaming** — the louder and higher your pitch, the further your chicken soars. Compete for the highest score on endlessly generated block layouts!

## Key Features

- **AR-Based Gameplay** — Interactive augmented reality mechanics that anchor the game world onto real flat surfaces using your phone's camera.
- **Voice Control** — Core gameplay driven entirely by the player's voice and screams; pitch detection translates your scream into in-game movement.
- **Procedural Generation** — Endless replayability with randomly generated block layouts every session.
- **Score Attack** — Run as long as possible and beat your personal best score.

## 📋 Prerequisites

- Android device with **ARCore support** (Android 7.0 or higher)
- A working **microphone** on your device
- Sufficient **lighting** in your environment for AR surface detection
- A **flat surface** nearby (table, floor, desk, etc.)

---

## ▶️ How to Run

Follow these steps to start playing **Screaming Chicken**:

**1. Install & Open the App**
   - Install the `.apk` file on your Android device.
   - Open the **Screaming Chicken** app.

**2. Press Start**
   - On the main menu, tap the **Start** button to begin a new game session.

**3. Find a Flat Surface**
   - Slowly pan your camera around the room.
   - Look for a **flat, well-lit surface** such as a table, desk, or floor.
   - Keep the camera steady and aimed at the surface.

**4. Place the Game**
   - When AR tracking detects a valid flat surface, a **placement indicator** (✦ reticle/marker) will appear on the surface.
   - **Tap the screen** on the indicator to place the game world onto that surface.

**5. Start Playing!**
   - Once the blocks are placed, the game begins automatically.
   - **Scream or shout** into your device's microphone to make your chicken jump.
   - The pitch and volume of your voice controls the movement speed and the height of your jump — scream louder and higher to reach further blocks!
   - Reach as many blocks as possible to achieve the **highest score**.

> 💡 **Tip:** Play in a quiet environment for more precise pitch detection. Make sure to grant microphone permission when prompted!
